Instructions

Encoding

To encode a url, type in what you need to encode e.g. “Do You Even Dencode Bro?”, click “Encode”, and result will look like this, “Do%20You%20Even%20Dencode%20Bro%3F”

Decoding

To decode a url, type in and elready encoded url e.g. “Do%20You%20Even%20Dencode%20Bro%3F”, click “Decode”, and result will look like normal again, “Do You Even Dencode Bro?”
